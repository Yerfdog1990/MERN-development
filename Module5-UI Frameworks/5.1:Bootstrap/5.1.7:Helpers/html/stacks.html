Stacks

Flexbox shorthand helpers to arrange components vertically or horizontally with built-in gap support.

Browser Note: gap utilities require Safari 14.1+ or modern versions of Chrome/Firefox.

1. Vertical Stack (.vstack)

Full-width Items
Top Item
Middle Item
Bottom Item
<!-- Items stack vertically and take full width -->
<div class="vstack gap-3">
  <div class="p-2">First item</div>
</div>

2. Horizontal Stack (.hstack)

Centered Alignment
Left
Pushed Right (ms-auto)
After VR
<!-- Use .ms-auto as a spacer and .vr for vertical rules -->
<div class="hstack gap-3">
  <div>Item 1</div>
  <div class="vr"></div>
  <div>Item 2</div>
</div>
