Viewport: Detecting...

Position Helpers

Quickly toggle between fixed and sticky positioning for layout components.

Note: Fixed elements are removed from the normal document flow and may overlap other content.

1. Fixed Positioning

Viewport Relative
.fixed-top stays at the very top of the screen.
.fixed-bottom stays at the very bottom of the screen.

Scroll the main page to see the "Viewport Tracker" in the corner—that's a Fixed element.

<!-- Edge to edge viewport positioning -->
<div class="fixed-top">...</div>
<div class="fixed-bottom">...</div>

2. Sticky Positioning

Flow Relative
I am sticky-top. Scroll me!
Scroll down to see the header stay at the top of this box. Sticky elements stay within their parent container until they "hit" the edge of the viewport.
<!-- Sticks only when it reaches the viewport edge -->
<div class="sticky-top">...</div>

3. Responsive Sticky

Breakpoint Aware

Only trigger sticky behavior at specific screen widths.

Sticky on MD and wider
Sticky on LG and wider
<!-- Responsive variants -->
<div class="sticky-sm-top">...</div>
<div class="sticky-xl-bottom">...</div>
