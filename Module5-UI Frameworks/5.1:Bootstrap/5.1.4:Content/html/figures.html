Bootstrap Figures

Combining images and captions into a single semantic component.

1. Basic Figure Anatomy

Uses .figure, .figure-img, and .figure-caption.

Standard Layout
<figure class="figure">
  <img class="figure-img img-fluid rounded">
  <figcaption class="figure-caption">...</figcaption>
</figure>

2. Caption Alignment

Alignment is controlled via standard text utilities on the figcaption.

Centered Caption
End (Right) Alignment
<figcaption class="figure-caption text-center">...</figcaption>
<figcaption class="figure-caption text-end">...</figcaption>

3. Figure with Picture Tag

Ensure .figure-img and .img-fluid are on the img tag inside.

<figure class="figure">
  <picture>
    <img class="figure-img img-fluid">
  </picture>
</figure>
