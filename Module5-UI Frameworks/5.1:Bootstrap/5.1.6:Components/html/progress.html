Progress

Custom progress bars featuring support for labels, stacked segments, and animated backgrounds.

V5.3 Update: The role="progressbar" and aria attributes now move to the .progress container.

1. Basic Structure & Accessibility

Container & Bar
25%

The blue dashed line is the .progress wrapper. The inner solid block is the .progress-bar.

<!-- Attributes on the wrapper for accessibility -->
<div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-valuenow="25">
  <!-- Width style on the inner bar -->
  <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 25%">25%</div>
</div>

2. Styling & Animation

Visual Feedback
<!-- Static Striped -->
<div class="progress-bar progress-bar-striped bg-warning">...</div>

<!-- Animated Stripes -->
<div class="progress-bar progress-bar-striped progress-bar-animated">...</div>

3. Stacked Progress

.progress-stacked

Note: When stacking, the width utility is applied to the .progress elements.

<div class="progress-stacked">
  <div class="progress" style="width: 15%">...</div>
  <div class="progress" style="width: 30%">...</div>
</div>

4. Custom Height

Vertical Scaling

Super Thin (1px)

Extra Thick (2.5rem)

<div class="progress" style="height: 20px">
  <div class="progress-bar">...</div>
</div>
