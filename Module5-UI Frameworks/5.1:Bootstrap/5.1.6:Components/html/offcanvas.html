Offcanvas

Hidden sidebars that toggle from any edge of the viewport via JavaScript.

1. Basic Structure & Toggle

Trigger & Container
Sidebar Menu

Any Bootstrap component can go here: links, forms, or images.

  • Menu Item 1
  • Menu Item 2
<!-- 1. The Trigger (Button or Link) -->
<button data-bs-toggle="offcanvas" data-bs-target="#ocBasic">Launch</button>

<!-- 2. The Panel (Hidden by default) -->
<div class="offcanvas offcanvas-start" id="ocBasic">
  <div class="offcanvas-header">...</div>
  <div class="offcanvas-body">...</div>
</div>

3. Backdrop & Scrolling

UX Behavior
No Backdrop
You can scroll the main page content while I am open!
<!-- Allow page scrolling while panel is open -->
<div data-bs-scroll="true" data-bs-backdrop="false">...</div>

<!-- Prevent closing when clicking outside -->
<div data-bs-backdrop="static">...</div>
